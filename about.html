Our Story

Baraka Car Rentals was founded in 2010 by Jack Baraka and his group of friends who saw a need for convenient and affordable transportation services in Nairobi and its environs. They combined their resources and ideas to start the company, starting with just a few vehicles, since they were passionate about offering outstanding customer service and were dedicated to quality. The business steadily expanded its fleet through hard work and dedication, earning a reputation as a dependable and trustworthy provider of rental automobiles in Nairobi and beyond.

Our Mission

At Baraka Car Rentals, our mission is to provide our customers with safe, reliable, and affordable rental cars, while delivering exceptional customer service every step of the way. We strive to make the car rental process as simple and convenient as possible, so that our customers can focus on enjoying their travels and making memories that last a lifetime.

Our Team

Our team at Baraka Car Rentals is made up of experienced and knowledgeable professionals who are dedicated to ensuring that our customers have the best possible car rental experience. From our friendly customer service representatives to our skilled mechanics and detailers, everyone at Baraka Car Rentals is committed to providing top-notch service and support.
